# Product Bets Shortlist

> Status: working draft

## Scoring model

Each bet should be scored 1–5 on:

1. Jones excitement / completion energy
2. 30–60 day paid V0 feasibility
3. AI / Jarvis leverage
4. Brand compounding for Jovicheer / JonesLab / Raijax
5. Product-line potential
6. Distribution clarity
7. Low support / admin burden

## Watch-outs

- Avoid physical products early: manufacturing, logistics, returns, customer support.
- Avoid large marketplace/platform cold-start as first revenue test.
- Avoid generic AI prompt packs unless connected to stronger brand/product line.
- Do not let research become a substitute for shipping.
